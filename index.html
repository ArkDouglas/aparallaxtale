A Parallax Tale

Something below

Scene 1: Wake-up

The protagonist wakes up in bed, eyes opening slowly as morning light filters through the window.

Scene 2: Leaving the House

He opens the door and steps outside into a world of green fields, leaving the house behind.

Scene 3: Walking towards the Cliff

The journey begins as he walks towards the distant cliff, the village visible below.

Scene 4: Looking over the Cliff

At the cliff's edge, he gazes down at the sprawling village nestled below the mist.

Scene 5: Starting the Descent

He begins to climb down the rocky walls, the path becoming more challenging as he progresses.

Scene 6: Meeting the Goose

A friendly goose appears, offering guidance and marking a safe path ahead.

Scene 7: Encountering the “Monster”

A large, furry monster emerges, initially intimidating but proves to be a helpful ally.

Scene 8: The Snake Encounter

A wise snake crosses his path, offering advice and revealing a hidden, safer route.

Scene 9: Trapped at the Bottom

Upon reaching the bottom, a sudden rockfall traps him, leaving him stranded.

Scene 10: Friends Come to the Rescue

The goose, monster, and snake unite to help him escape the precarious situation.

Scene 11: The Ascent

Together, they climb back up the cliff, overcoming obstacles with teamwork and determination.

Scene 12: Back at the Top, Reflecting

At the summit, they reflect on their journey, gazing out over the peaceful village as the sun sets.
